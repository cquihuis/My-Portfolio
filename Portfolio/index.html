Some Things About Me


I currently work as a jewelry consultant in South Texas, and I spend a lot of time learning about gemstones and diamonds. In general, I've always loved to learn, and a lot of what I've learned becomes a medium for self-expression in some way. My strongest form of self-expression is simply writing. For this reason, I'm pretty good at it!

In terms of hobbies, I love learning about cooking and baking! Videos, cookbooks, articles, word-of-mouth, I use all of those things to be able to learn more recipes. The process of preparing ingredients and mixing them all in certain proportions and in certain ways is meditative for me. I also love having my family and friends eat and enjoy the finished product. Both cooking and baking can be a lot of trial and error (like coding!), and it gets frustrating sometimes, but that's just what comes with learning.
